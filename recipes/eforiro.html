Efo Riro Recipes

Description

Efo Riro is a type of soup commonly found in the South-west part of Nigeria. Yes, the Yorubas are popular for their efo riro. Even though it can be eaten with swallow and rice, the yorubas are fond of eating it with pounded yam. It is mostly served in special occasions like wedding parties, birthdays, burials and many others because of the richness of the ingredients it's cooked with

Ingredients

  • Efo
  • Pepper and onions
  • Palm Oil
  • Seasoning cubes and Salt
  • Ponmo
  • Stockfish and Cray Fish

Steps

  1. Chop the efo into fine pieces, blend the pepper and onions
  2. Pre heat the Palm oil
  3. Pour the blended pepper and onion into the pot of preheated palm oil, leave to fry for 5 minutes
  4. Add stockfish and Crayfish, add desired seasoning and allow to cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add the chopped efo and leave to cook for 15 minutes
