Jollof Okra (Ila Asepo) Recipes

Description

Okra is a type of soup found all over Nigeria. It is eaten with swallow, it's a very common soup but still craved. It is drawy and very delicious in nature. Different cultures have different styles of cooking Okra. In this article, I'll be showing you how to cook the Jollof Okra, which is also known as Ila Asepo. It's the type of okra that doesn't need stew as its complement in meals

Ingredients

  • Okra
  • Pepper and onions
  • Palm Oil
  • Seasoning cubes and Salt
  • Ponmo
  • Stockfish and Cray Fish

Steps

    Do not cover pot in this process
  1. Blend the Okra desired smoothness, blend the pepper and onions
  2. Pre heat the Palm oil for two minutes
  3. Add the okra and leave to cook for 5 minutes
  4. Pour the blended pepper and onion into the pot of okra, leave to cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add stockfish and Crayfish, add desired seasoning and allow to cook for 8 minutes
